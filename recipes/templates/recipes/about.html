Hey there!

I go by JD and I'm a Security Engineer based out of Charlotte, NC. I'm currently approaching my 5th year in the security industry and in that time I have found a passion for the work I do.

My entry into security began with a cybersecurity bootcamp, Evolve Security Academy. There, I learned foundational skills crucial to success in security. I had the chance to explore some of the many different domains in this field; this list includes network security, malware analysis, infrastructure as code (IaC), and pentesting to name a few. It's here that I developed an interest in vulnerability management and the endless possiblities of developing automated solutions.

Now you may be wondering how my journey led me to creating a web development portfolio. Let me explain...

According to Verizon's 2024 Data Breach Investigations Report (DBIR), web apps have become the second most common attack pattern. This means it's hard to avoid web applications when it comes to vulnerability management. With this being said, I chose to take up web development to give myself a better understanding of web related vulnerabilities.

When I'm not working I love to get out on a run, or on a trail with my pup and partner.

Feel free to take a look at my profile to see some of my other work. If you'd like to connect or discuss a project, don't hesitate to reach out!

Thanks for dropping in!
